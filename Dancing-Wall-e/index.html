Meet Wall-E, a dancing version that will dance to a metronome beat you provide.

This is the PIE final project of Karen, Kelly, Jules, Jong Ho and Sydney (learn more on our about us page).

Our main objective was to create a Wall-E replica. Instead of re-creating the trash compaction functionality, we wanted to instead incorporate an aspect of music visualization. The method we ultimately settled on was dance, in which Wall-E would interpret a BPM based on audio input and move its limbs to the beat.
